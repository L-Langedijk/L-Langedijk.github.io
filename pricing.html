Pricing

I follow the CATS pricing structure:

  • Free 20 minute initial consultation – find out if you want to continue with this therapist.
  • Pay-as-you-can, with market rate being £60 per session

See full CATS pricing information here: https://www.creativeartstherapiesspace.org/referral


Financial Aid

The CATS pricing structure means that you can choose the price to suit you financial position (for time-limited therapy). Here are some terms to search that may help you find financial aid to cover the cost of therapy:

  • Self-Directed Support (SDS)
  • Pupil Equity Funding (PEF)
