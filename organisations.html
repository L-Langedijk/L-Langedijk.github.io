Organisations

Every organization has unique therapeutic requirements. To ensure I can best support your organization’s specific needs, I invite you to share a brief overview of your requirements.

Examples of Common Services Provided

Psycho-Education

Outcome: Building resilience and personal skills of your community and stakeholders.

  • Mental health awareness content production: printed/digital media or workshops.

Professional Development & Staff Support

Outcome: Improve staff retention, decrease lost efficiency in organisational change, and help staff to process the demands of their roles.

  • Burnout Prevention Workshops: Creative interventions designed to reduce compassion fatigue and improve staff retention.
  • Team Building & Culture Transitions: Using art-based directives to improve communication, navigate organizational change, or integrate new teams.
  • Reflective Practice Groups: Facilitated sessions for frontline workers (social workers, medical staff, etc.) to process the emotional demands of their roles.

Clinical & Client-Facing Programs

Outcome: Personal or collective processing leading to improvement of self.

  • Themed Therapeutic Groups: Time-limited sessions (e.g., 6–12 weeks) focusing on specific issues like anxiety, bereavement, or neurodiversity.
  • On-Site Individual Art Therapy: Providing regular, dedicated hours for one-on-one sessions within your organisation.
  • Crisis Intervention Support: Creative debriefing sessions following a significant organizational or community event.
